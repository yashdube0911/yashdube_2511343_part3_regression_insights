# Residual Analysis

Finally, the multiple regression model was used to calculate residuals.

## Largest Positive Residuals

The five largest positive residuals represent stores that made many more sales than expected in their month.

Possible reasons include:

- Successful local promotions
- Strong store management
- Seasonal demand
Increased website visitors: more than anticipated.

## Largest Negative Residuals

The top five residuals are negative, meaning the monthly sales for these stores were lower than expected.

Possible reasons include:

- Local competition
- Temporary operational issues
- Poor product availability
The storage factors are not measured in the model.

## Overall Assessment

Overall, most residuals seem to be reasonably distributed, indicating good overall fit of the model. But, some stores do much better or much worse than the model, and there may be other variables that are responsible for even greater predictive power.

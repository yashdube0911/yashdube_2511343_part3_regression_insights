## Business Problem

This project aims to find out what are the most significant factors of business that contribute to the sales performance of the retail stores on a monthly basis.

---

## Dataset

We have the sales data along with marketing, customer, operational and regional data available at monthly level.

Dependent Variable:

- Monthly Sales

Independent Variables:

- Marketing Spend
- Footfall
- Inventory Availability
- Customer Rating
- Region (Dummy Variable)

---

## Regression Approach

Two simple regression models and one multiple regression model were created.

Dummy variables for the Region variable were generated with North as the reference group.

---

## Model Comparison

Marketing Spend Model

- R² = 0.167

Footfall Model

- R² = 0.736

Multiple Regression Model

- R² = 0.817

Multiple regression model was chosen as final model.

---

## Business Recommendation

More customers, better stocking, ensuring customer satisfaction and increasing the investment in marketing strategies are probably some of the ways to boost monthly revenue.

---

## Assumptions & Limitations

Association is the concept of regression, not causation.

There may be other variables outside of the data set that can affect store performance.
